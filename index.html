Tomb

Tomb of the Past is one of the first first-person shooter (FPS) maps I made. It was an ambitious map which takes heavy inspiration from Quake maps. The goal of the map is to find the ancient king's treasure trove. This goal will have you spelunking through this tomb filled with traps, secrets, monsters, and even researchers and soldiers that arrived before you did.

Tomb is designed to be a non-linear map where the player can progress the multiple paths in whichever order they so choose. Given that it’s a burial site for a king, there are many secrets to be found within the different chambers.

The tomb is also inhabited by researchers and their accompanying soldiers. The researchers are also looking for a way to gain passage into the King’s burial chamber, but have had no luck so far.

This maze is found later in the map when you fall down a trap door and have to make your way back to the main chamber.

This is the main hall where you need to present to the alter the true-king's birthright in order to gain access to his final resting place.

Deep Sea

Deep Sea is an underwater mining facility map that also doubles as a research center for otherworldly beings found in the deepest parts of the Earth.

The goal of the map is to get to the central command of the current floor in order to gain access to the next floor. In order to do that you need to find multiple keycards guarded by top agents. Throughout the level you may even encounter monsters that have broken loose from their test tubes and cages.

Jungle

Jungle is a small map that takes place in the heart of the rain forest. A civilization lost to time is hidden away and the adventurer has come looking for relics that promise powers beyond imagination.

Jungle is a linear experience which has you learning the basics of combat for a much longer campaign of levels. It functions as the first level and introduces multiple enemy types and various ancient relics that you find throughout the ruins and caves.

Survival

Survival is wave-based zombie survival map. It takes place in a small, abandoned clinic. However, underneath is a teleporter that will get you to a safer location. Problem is, it doesn’t work.

The map is quite small in order to emphasize the difficulty of surviving a zombie apocalypse. Between multiple waves of zombies will be calm moments where you can take the time to explore the map and find ways to bring the teleporter back online in order to get out. Don’t take too longer however or you may get overrun by zombies!

About Me

I love video games. It is the medium in which I find the most enjoyment and the artform I would like to use to express myself in.

Video games have been a major part of my life since the age of eight when I received my first videogame console the Nintendo GameCube bundled with Mario Party 7. It was during high school I became interested in game development as my future career. Since then, I've been working towards strengthening my skill set and building a portfolio as a game developer.
